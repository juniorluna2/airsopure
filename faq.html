Why should I have my air ducts cleaned?

The health benefits alone are a convincing reason to have your air ducts cleaned. People with asthma and allergies will notice the biggest changes in their air quality.

Will my home be cleaner after having my duct work cleaned?

Most home owners notice they dust less often- that the vents appear cleaner longer.

When should I have my air ducts cleaned?

Any time of the year is a good time to improve the quality in your home.

How often should I have my air ducts cleaned?

The EPA recommends that the average home have its ductwork cleaned every 7 to 10 years. Every 5 to 7 if you have smokers or pets that shed.

Why do you ask about the square footage of my home rather than the number of vents my home has?

Our prices are based on square footage as we know approximately how many linear feet of duct work it takes to adequately heat your home.

Why do you include a blower assembly cleaning with an air duct cleaning?

While removing debris from your system, some of that dust and dirt falls into your furnace. We need to clean your blower so when you turn on your furnace, the debris doesn't blow through your home.

Do you recommend paying the extra money to have the system sanitized?

Not sanitizing the system is like doing only half the job- that's why we include it for no extra charge with every duct cleaning we do.

Why do you provide digital before and after pictures of the job?

Pictures don't lie. How can you prove the job is done right if no one else can see what you really did?

What kind of maintenance do I need to do after the job if done?

We recommend using standard 1'' paper pleated filters over the traditional fiber glass. Fiber glass filters are only 7% effective where paper pleated 42 to 48% effective depending on manufacturer. These should be changed per your furnace manufacturers' specifications- generally, once a month.
